Journey Through Culture, Tradition & Nature's Wisdom

Explore the journey of the palm tree through culture, tradition, and nature's wisdom. From ancient roots to modern relevance, this story brings the legacy to life for all ages.

All parts of the palm tree are useful to mankind ‚Äî leaves, fruits, stem, petiole, and inflorescence. Nothing goes to waste. Our ancestors honored it as the world's "Karpaga Virutcham."

Native to the Indian subcontinent, the palm tree has played an economic, religious, medicinal, and environmental role in ancient Tamil Nadu. Recognizing its importance, the Government of Tamil Nadu declared it the state tree in 1978.
