What is Padhaneer?

Padhaneer/Neera is a natural, sweet sap collected from palmyra trees. It is 100% organic, rich in essential vitamins and minerals, and serves as a healthy alternative to sugary soft drinks. Traditionally consumed in Coastal India, Padhaneer is not just a beverage—it’s a cultural heritage that supports both wellness and local livelihoods.

Harvested at dawn and consumed fresh, Padhaneer is known for its cooling properties and energizing effects. It is nature’s own energy drink, untouched by chemicals or preservatives.
