What is PadhaNeer?

Nature's Pure Energy from Ancient Palmyra Trees

Padhaneer/Neera is a natural, sweet sap collected from palmyra trees. It is 100% organic, rich in essential vitamins and minerals, and serves as a healthy alternative to sugary soft drinks. Traditionally consumed in Coastal India, Padhaneer is not just a beverageâ€”it's a cultural heritage that supports both wellness and local livelihoods.

100% Natural

Harvested at dawn and consumed fresh, Padhaneer is known for its cooling properties and energizing effects. It is nature's own energy drink, untouched by chemicals or preservatives.

Health Benefits

Rich in essential vitamins, minerals, and natural sugars that provide sustained energy while supporting overall health and wellness.

Cultural Heritage

The palm is significant in Indian culture, with the Palmyra palm being the official tree of Tamil Nadu, considered a "celestial tree" because every part is useful.
